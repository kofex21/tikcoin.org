Invariants (Canon)

  • Core token: TIK (ERC-20, decimals=0, totalSupply=3,155,760,000), no owner/mint/pause/blacklist.
  • No bridges, no cross-chain. Optional limited side tokens only via 3/5 multisig under strict policy.
  • Distribution: Airdrop #1 = 1% (31,557,600 TIK). Community pool = 20%. All locks & vestings are on-chain and transparent.
  • Governance: Tiered-DAO + Timelock + Policy Module + Multisig Executor (3/5) + Constitutional Guardian (pre-execution veto; no treasury spend rights).
  • Symbolism sequence is fixed everywhere: Sun ‚Üí Hourglass ‚Üí Mechanical ‚Üí Quartz ‚Üí Tourbillon ‚Üí Atom ‚Üí Quantum.
  • Mainnet only. Listing/liquidity per public playbook. Open-source repos + audits published.

FAQ (Essentials)

  • Why decimals=0? Time is indivisible at unit granularity for this design; it simplifies accounting and UX.
  • Why no bridges? Attack surface & trust minimization. Side tokens only via 3/5 multisig if strictly necessary.
  • Taxes/Burns? None. All flows are explicit: locks, vestings, grants.
  • Airdrop #1 size? 1% of total supply = 31,557,600 TIK, equivalent to one year in seconds.
