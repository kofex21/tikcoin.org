3.11 Roadmap — In Plain Words

We keep our roadmap simple and honest. No buzzwords, no hidden promises. Below is what we plan to deliver before listing. Beyond that, only one principle applies: Time Will Tell.

V1 — Prototype (☀️ Sun)

  • Deploy prototype contracts: TIKP (1 TIK = 1 second, cap = 31,557,600), AirdropDistributor, multisig 3/5.
  • Launch Airdrop #1 with referral ladder (≤33, from community pool).
  • Immutable ethos: no bridges, no admin tricks, no hidden switches.

Result: the idea goes live — time cannot be faked.

V1.5 — Presale Prep (⏳ Hourglass)

  • Presale contract with anti-sybil filters (Gitcoin Passport / WorldID threshold, commit-reveal).
  • Vesting: 10% unlock at TGE, 2-month cliff, 90% linear over 18 months (fully unlocked by month 20).
  • LaunchEscrow: stores presale funds, enforces $0.01 listing, anyone can call launch() at deadline. LP tokens go directly to timelock (≥12m).
  • Fund split: $6M → liquidity (locked), $3M → reserve (timelock), $2M → development.

Result: a fair presale, permissionless launch, no “trust me”.

V2 — Immutable Core & Listing (💎 Quartz)

  • Deploy final TIK (immutable ERC20, supply = 3,155,760,000, no mint/pause/tax/blacklist, ownership = 0x0).
  • Migrate 1:1 from TIKP via Migrator.
  • Permissionless listing at $0.01 (from LaunchEscrow). LP tokens already locked.
  • Governance: multisig 3/5 + Timelock + Constitutional Guardian.
  • Utilities live:
    • Century Meter: on-chain dashboard of the century’s progress.
    • NFT Clocks: dynamic by share of locked tokens.

Result: immutable core, liquidity locked, utilities online.

V3 — Ecosystem Growth (🌀 Tourbillon → ⚛️ Atom)

  • DAO votes with tiered governance, referrals and dashboards on-chain.
  • DEX and aggregator integrations.
  • Partnerships and creative utilities — only if aligned with the canon (no bridges, no admin privileges).

Result: the century evolves. Beyond this point — Time Will Tell.

Summary: V1 = idea and airdrop, V1.5 = fair presale (anti-sybil, escrow, vesting), V2 = immutable core & listing, V3 = ecosystem growth. After that — Time Will Tell.
