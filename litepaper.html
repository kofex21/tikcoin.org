Litepaper v1.0

Foundation

Immutable ERC-20 core. No admin keys. No burns. No stealth taxes.

Token Allocation

  • 35% — Presale
  • 20% — Liquidity (locked per listing plan)
  • 20% — Community & Growth (single pool: presale bonuses 10/20/30%; remainder for referrals, memes, content, collabs)
  • 15% — Marketing (multisig + timelock; monthly on-chain reports)
  • 10% — Founder (see Unlock Policy)

Presale

Open-ended (no fixed end date). Listing occurs on day 8 after sell-out.

Unlock Policy — Overview

  • Tier 0 — Sun: unlocked at TGE.
  • Tier 1 — Hourglass: unlocked at TGE.
  • Tiers 2–4 (Mechanical • Tourbillon • Eternity): months 1–5 → 10% each (50% total), months 6–7 → none, month 8 → remaining 50%.
  • Whales (top bracket): months 1–5 → 10% each; month 7 → 25%, month 8 → 25%.
  • Founder 10%: 10% at TGE; remaining 90% start vesting only after all presale participants complete their unlocks (default: linear 12 months; adjustable before contract freeze).

Vision

We don’t value hype. We value time.
