1. Overview

TIKcoin is a mechanism of trust: minimal immutable ERC‑20 base, transparent distribution, public locks, no admin keys. Utilities are external modules.

2. Principles

  • Honesty over hype
  • Fair, immutable base
  • No taxes / burns / blacklists
  • On‑chain receipts

3. Distribution (simplified)

  • 35% — Presale
  • 20% — Liquidity
  • 20% — Community & Growth Pool (bonuses + referrals/memes)
  • 15% — Marketing (future, multisig + timelock)
  • 10% — Founder (strict unlocks)

4. Presale & TGE

No calendar end-date; presale ends at sell-out. TGE & initial listing open on day 8 after sell‑out, enforced by smart contracts. Per-wallet hard cap.

5. Security

No admin keys on the token; audits in phases; public bounty post‑TGE. Treasury under multisig + timelock.

6. Roadmap

Genesis → Growth → Momentum → Eternity (v2 will include dates and milestones).
