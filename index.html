Genesis & Ethos

TIKcoin starts simple and grows organically. We’re not recruiting “investors”; we’re attracting builders — people who value clear rules and consistent behavior. The base stays minimal and immutable. Everything else is layered on top, transparently.

  • Honesty. Speak plainly, publish what matters, keep receipts.
  • Fairness. Equal conditions. No privileged backdoors.
  • Immutability. Base rules are fixed. Extensions never break the foundation.

Why TIKcoin

  • Safe foundation. A clean ERC‑20 contract without taxes, burns or blacklists.
  • Transparent distribution. Allocations and locks are fixed and published in advance.
  • Evolution safeguard. Optional 1:1 migration via multisig — only if it protects participants.
