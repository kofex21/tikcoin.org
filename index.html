About TIKcoin

What we’re building

TIKcoin is a community-first crypto experiment grounded in radical honesty. We publish the rules before launch, lock the core, and keep incentives simple. We don’t chase hype — we respect time.

Our aim is to let participation feel fair: transparent tokenomics, clear liquidity, and vesting that respects both early believers and long-term stability.

Principles

  • No burns, no stealth taxes
  • Immutable core (whitelisted improvements only)
  • Community incentives without supply abuse
  • Open-ended presale → listing on day 8 after sell-out

FAQ

Are you “monetizing” time?

No. Our framing is different: we tokenize time-aligned participation and behavior. The mechanics stay auditable and fair.

Any burns or hidden taxes?

No. Fixed supply. No burns. No stealth taxes.

When do you list?

Eight days after the presale sells out. The countdown starts at sell-out, not before.

How does vesting work for tiers?

Sun & Hourglass: unlocked at TGE. Mechanical • Tourbillon • Eternity: months 1–5 → 10% each (50%), months 6–7 → none, month 8 → remaining 50%. Top-bracket wallets (“whales”): months 1–5 → 10% each; month 7 → 25%; month 8 → 25%.

How is the founder allocation locked?

Founder 10%: 10% at TGE (same as everyone). The remaining 90% begin vesting only after all presale participants finish their unlocks (default: linear 12 months; finalized before contract freeze).

What’s in the community pool (20%)?

Presale bonuses (10/20/30%) plus the remainder for referrals, memes, content, and collaborations — under transparent controls.
