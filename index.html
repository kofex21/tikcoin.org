Doctrine of Time

1) No hidden functions

Rules are public and final before launch. No stealth mints, no black boxes.

2) Immutable core

The core contract is frozen. Only whitelisted upgrades that preserve the foundation.

3) Transparent vesting

Simple schedules on-chain. Early trust is respected, late stability is preserved.

4) One honest community pool

Single 20% pool for bonuses/referrals/memes/collabs with on-chain controls.

5) Markets over hype

Liquidity and listing by a published playbook. No surprise taxes or burns.

6) Same rules for founder

Founder vesting mirrors community logic; no shortcuts, no special exits.

FAQ

No. We tokenize time-aligned participation and behavior. The mechanics stay auditable and fair.

No. Fixed supply. No burns. No stealth taxes.

When do you list?

Eight days after the presale sells out. The countdown starts at sell-out, not before.

How does vesting work for tiers?

Sun & Hourglass: unlocked at TGE. Mechanical • Quartz • Tourbillon: months 1–5 → 10% each (50%), months 6–7 → none, month 8 → remaining 50%. Whale bracket: months 1–5 → 10% each; month 7 → 25%; month 8 → 25%.

How is the founder allocation locked?

Founder 10%: 10% at TGE (same as everyone). The remaining 90% begin vesting only after all presale participants finish their unlocks (final params fixed before contract freeze).
