About TIKcoin

What we’re building

TIKcoin is a community-first crypto experiment grounded in radical honesty. We publish the rules before launch, lock the core, and keep incentives simple. We don’t chase hype — we cultivate trust.

Our vision is to make participation feel fair: transparent tokenomics, clear liquidity, and vesting that respects both early believers and long-term stability.

Principles

  • No burns, no stealth taxes
  • Immutable core (whitelisted improvements only)
  • Community incentives without supply abuse
  • Open-ended presale → listing on day 8 after sell-out

FAQ

Is there a burn or hidden tax?

No. Fixed supply, no burns, no stealth taxes. We keep the mechanics clean and auditable.

When does listing happen?

Eight days after the presale sells out. This is stated upfront for predictability and fairness.

How does vesting work for tiers?

Sun & Hourglass: unlocked at TGE. Mechanical • Tourbillon • Eternity: months 1–5 unlock 10% each (50%), months 6–7 no unlocks, month 8 unlocks the remaining 50%. Top-bracket wallets (“whales”): months 1–5 → 10% each; month 7 → 25%; month 8 → 25%.

How is the founder allocation locked?

Founder 10%: 10% unlock at TGE (same as everyone), the remaining 90% begin vesting only after all presale participants complete their unlocks (default plan: linear vesting over 12 months starting the following month; final cadence is fixed before contract freeze).

What’s in the community pool (20%)?

Presale bonuses (10/20/30%) plus the remainder for referrals, memes, content, and collaborations — managed transparently.

Why “open-ended” presale?

We prefer demand-led timing. Community heat matters more than arbitrary dates. The listing countdown starts the moment we sell out.
