    • Months 6–7: no unlocks (market stabilization).
    • Month 8: remaining 50% in one tranche.
  • Whales (top-bracket wallets at the upper buy cap):
    • Months 1–5: 10% each (total 50%).
    • Month 7: 25%, Month 8: 25% (still complete within 8 months with a softer market impact).
  • Founder 10%:
    • 5% — unlocks one year after all presale participants finish their unlocks.
    • 5% — unlocks once the project reaches Top-50 by market cap (or by a hard fallback date if that metric becomes disputable; finalized before contract freeze).

Note: exact “whale” threshold is fixed in the contract/presale rules. All periods are calendar months from TGE.
