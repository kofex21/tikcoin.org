Breakdown & Locks

  • Presale (35%). Hard wallet cap per address to avoid whales. Vesting/claim schedule published before TGE.
  • Liquidity (20%). 85% added at TGE; 15% under timelock for price stabilization.
  • Airdrop (20%). 15% reserved for 10/20/30% buyer bonuses (locked ≥12 months). 5% operational pool for referrals/memes/collabs under multisig, on‑chain tracked.
  • Founder (10%). 50% unlocks one year after community unlock completion; remaining 50% unlocks after the project reaches Top‑50 by market cap (on a major tracker). Immutable rule.
  • Marketing (future) (15%). Locked under multisig + timelock; spend only for listings/brand‑level campaigns. On‑chain proposals & receipts required.

Governance Safeguards

  • Multisig + Timelock. Treasury actions require N-of-M signatures and a public delay.
  • No admin keys in the token. The base ERC‑20 is immutable. All utilities live in external modules.
  • Emergency 1:1 migration (optional). Only via multisig and with an opt‑in swap contract.
