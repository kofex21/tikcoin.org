Introduction

TIKCOIN is not another meme-token. We anchor value in the most universal unit — time. We tokenize one century — specifically the XXI century. The basic unit is a second: 1 TIK = 1 second. Total supply equals the seconds in 100 years: 3,155,760,000 TIK (decimals=0).

Three axioms guide every mechanism:

  1. Time cannot be stopped. — The core token is immutable; no pause switches.
  2. Time cannot be reversed. — No upgrade that alters fundamentals after freeze.
  3. Time cannot be deceived. — No hidden taxes, no stealth mint, no backdoors.

We choose minimalism: Ethereum mainnet only, no bridges, open repositories, published audits, transparent locks and vestings. Marketing follows meaning — not the other way around.

Chapter 2 — Honesty

2.1 Honest Code

  • Open-source: repos public; reproducible builds; tagged releases.
  • No hidden functions: no stealth mint; no taxes; no blacklist/pause in V2.
  • Independent audits: audit PDFs published; hash in repo; issues tracked openly.
  • No bridges / no backdoors: attack surface minimized by design.

2.2 Honest Airdrop

  • Time-first distribution: rewards for verified contributions (content, support, referrals).
  • Sybil resistance: e.g., Passport/WorldID + off-chain scoring → Merkle root → on-chain claim with proof.
  • Funding source: only from Community pool (20%); Airdrop #1 = 1%.

2.3 Honest Presale (IOU)

  • Model: selling future TIK (IOU) via audited LaunchEscrow; funds release per thresholds; refunds if unmet.
  • Price: target $0.01 per future TIK (policy-controlled; final terms published pre-launch).
  • Compliance & risk: clear disclosures; geo policy; on-chain dashboard for inflows.
  • Bonuses: only from Community pool; vesting applies equally.

2.4 Vesting

  • Universal schedule (baseline): 10% at TGE, 2-month cliff, then 90% linearly over 18 months (full unlock by month 20).
  • LP & reserves: LP tokens locked ≥18 months; reserve tokens timelocked; injections only by DAO at market terms.
  • Vaults: labeled addresses; stricter cliffs permitted (e.g., 12-month cliff + 24–36 mo linear) for protocol vaults.
