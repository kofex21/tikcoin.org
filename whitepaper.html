Introduction

1 TIK = 1 second. Total supply: 3,155,760,000 TIK — exactly one century of seconds. Slogan: “We cannot stop time — but we can tokenize it.”

Our aim is simple: align effort, honesty, and time. TIKCOIN encodes time as the only universal unit and structures distribution so that early, committed contributors receive proportionally higher recognition — without compromising equality of rules.

1. Tokenomics

Total supply: 3,155,760,000 TIK (1 TIK = 1 second).

  • 35% — Presale (price $0.01 per TIK)
  • 20% — Airdrop (incl. contribution & presale-bonus payouts)
  • 20% — Reserve (future development)
  • 10% — Marketing (locked)

Use of presale funds: 85% → liquidity; 15% → development (audits, contract upgrades, infrastructure). Levels are assigned only during presale and cannot be gained or lost afterwards.

2. Honesty Doctrine

Honesty is encoded through transparent rules, consistent vesting for all locked categories, and public reporting. We prioritize clarity over hype and commit to irreversible constraints when they increase user trust.

3. Equality of Time

Time is the only universal unit. All accounting and rewards derive from time. Equal categories share the same vesting rules; voting weight maps to the same unit across the system.

4. Time Axioms

  • Time cannot be stopped → No arbitrary pauses of vesting or distributions.
  • Time cannot be reversed → No rollbacks or retroactive changes to finalized allocations.
  • Time cannot be cheated → Anti-sybil, immutable rules, audits, on-chain verifiability.

6. Contract Mechanics

  • V1: TIKP + Distributor for airdrop/bonus accounting; minimal surface.
  • V1.5: Telegram bot: onboarding, proofs, payouts, notifications.
  • V2: Immutable TIK; audit; DEX readiness.
  • V3: Integrations and first CEX listing.
  • V4–V6: Collaborations, NFT roles/utility, Quantum Clock.

7. Presale

  • Price: $0.01 per TIK.
  • Cap per wallet: $50,000.
  • No extensions. Listing occurs on the 8th day after closure (date not pre-announced).
  • Levels determined strictly during presale.

8. Presale Bonuses

Bonuses are paid only from the Airdrop allocation; never sold. Final bonus % is computed at presale end based on contributed amount and level reached.

  • Mechanic 2.5% • Quartz 5% • Tourbillon 10% • Atomic 20% • Quantum 30%

9. Airdrop (time → tokens)

Community tasks and contributions convert time to tokens at 1:1 logic (within program limits). Rewards are paid exclusively from the Airdrop pool and never sold by the project.

10. Vesting & Lockups

  • Unified vesting logic for all locked categories to preserve equality.
  • Founder unlocks 12 months after the global unlock.
  • On-chain timelocks; no “backdoors” or privileged bypasses.

11. Governance & Voting

Voting rights: Tourbillon, Atomic, Quantum. Topics: lock periods, roadmap priorities, integrations. One unit of time maps to one unit of voting weight.

12. Utility

  • Telegram bot for onboarding, proofs, payouts, and status.
  • NFT roles, partner access, and future merchant integrations.

13. Symbolism & Golden Age

Seven stages (Sun → Hourglass → Mechanic → Quartz → Tourbillon → Atomic → Quantum) form our visual language and educational path. “Golden Age” names our tokenized century.

14. Communications

Wave-based releases (no fixed public dates), periodic reports, and transparent KPIs for community growth, development progress, and treasury usage (85/15 split from presale respected).

15. Risks & Disclaimer

Markets are volatile; technical and regulatory landscapes change. This document is not financial advice. Users must perform their own due diligence and comply with local laws.

Conclusion

Time is the only universal unit. TIKCOIN encodes time, rewards contribution, and compounds honesty into long-term value. We cannot stop time — but together we can tokenize it.
