FAQ

Why decimals = 0?

Time is atomic at the chosen granularity. decimals=0 simplifies accounting and UX.

Why no bridges?

To minimize attack surface and trust. Optional side tokens only via 3/5 multisig under strict policy.

Are there taxes or burns?

No. All flows are explicit: locks, vestings, grants. No stealth mechanics.

What is Airdrop #1?

1% of total supply (31,557,600 TIK), conceptually equivalent to one year in seconds; distributed by verifiable contribution.

When listing?

Listing follows the public playbook after V2 & policy checkpoints; LP lock proofs and addresses are published.
